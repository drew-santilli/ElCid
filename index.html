El Cid

legendary Spainish Warrior

Here is a Timeline for El Cid's Life

  • c. 1043 born Rodrigo Díaz, Vivar, Spain
  • c. 1057 El Cid fights against the Moores at their stronghold, Zaragoza.
  • c. 1063 El Cid fights in the Battle of Graus, as a champion for Ferdinand the Great
  • c. 1079 Battle of Cabra
  • c. 1080 El Cid is exiled
  • c. 1081 El Cid enters the service of Moorish king, Yusuf al-Mu'taman ibn Hud.
  • c. 1084 El Cid defeats the Aragonese, with the help of the Army of the Taifa of Zaragoza.
  • c. 1086 The Almoravid invade the Iberian Peninsula.
  • c. 1087 El Cid is recalled by Alfonso, and sent to negotiate with the Taifa kingdom.
  • c. 1090 El Cid defeated and captured Berenguer in the Battle of Tébar.
  • c. 1092 A seige begins on Valencia lead by El Cid.
  • c. 1093 Failed attempt to break seige.
  • c. 1094 El Cid wins and controls the Mediterranean.
  • c. 1099 El Cid dies.
